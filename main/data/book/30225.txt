I'm not alone, I'm not alone, I'm not alone. 

I've been hearing voices; whispers that keep telling me there's no hope. 

My ears are ringing; the hairs on my arm are perpetually on-end.
The darkness is settling in around me more and more.
It's grasping at my limbs and teasing me into its acceptance. 

I just feel...compelled to keep staring at the door. There's also a noticeable smell of...
decay... in the air. 

Something is here in this house with me. And it knows I'm here too. 
I'm not leaving this room on my own...
